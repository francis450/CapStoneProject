International Fight Week (IFW) is an annual event that takes place in Las Vegas, Nevada, and is dedicated to celebrating the sport of mixed martial arts (MMA). The first International Fight Week was held in July 2013, and since then it has become a staple event in the MMA community.

International Fight Week Logo

The International Fight Week (IFW) logo was created by the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) design team. The logo features the image of a clenched fist in front of a globe, with the words "International Fight Week" written in bold lettering. According to the UFC, the logo was designed to represent the spirit of competition and the global reach of the sport of mixed martial arts (MMA). The clenched fist is a symbol of strength and determination, while the globe represents the worldwide appeal of MMA.
