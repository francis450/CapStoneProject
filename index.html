INTERNATIONAL
FIGHT WEEK 2023

During International Fight Week, the UFC hosts various events, including fan experiences, open workouts, press conferences, and culminating with a major MMA event featuring top fighters from around the world. The event attracts thousands of MMA fans from around the world and is a celebration of the sport and its culture.

2023.07.03 (MON) ~ 09 (SUN)

@ T-Mobile Arena, Las Vegas, Nevada

Main Program

Conference

During the conference, fighters, will gather to discuss upcoming fights, make announcements, and interact with fans.

Exhibition

Exhibition matches will take many forms, ranging from grappling and sparring sessions to full-blown MMA fights with modified rules.

Interviews

Fighters will promote the fights, share their thoughts about their opponents, and answer questions from the media and fans.

Commentators will typically provide commentary and insight on the fighter's technique, discussing their strengths and weaknesses.
